Platform Overview

Welcome to the Customer Portal — your centralised hub for managing permissions, licences, payments, and account activity.

1. Dashboard

Upon signing in, you will be presented with the main dashboard providing at-a-glance summaries of your active permissions, pending applications, recent activity, and upcoming renewal dates.

  • Active Permissions — view all current permissions with status indicators
  • Pending Applications — track submissions awaiting review
  • Recent Activity — timeline of actions taken in the last 30 days
  • Renewal Alerts — upcoming expiration dates flagged for attention

2. Navigation Structure

The platform uses a consistent navigation pattern across all sections.

• Stable internet (min 2 Mbps)
• Resolution 1280×720+
• JavaScript enabled

You will receive an activation email within 24 hours of registration.
