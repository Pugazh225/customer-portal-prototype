    Terms and Conditions

    1. Application Terms
    By submitting this suspension application, you agree to abide by the terms set forth by the local authority for the temporary reservation of parking bays.

    2. Payment
    All fees are non-refundable once the suspension signs have been placed. Cancellation before sign placement may be eligible for a partial refund subject to council policy.

    3. Duration
    The suspension is valid only for the dates and times specified in your application. Any extension must be applied for separately.

    4. Compliance
    You are responsible for ensuring the suspended area is used only for the stated purpose. Misuse may result in penalties or revocation.

    5. Liability
    The council is not liable for any vehicles parked in the suspended area prior to sign placement or for any damage to vehicles during the suspension period.

    Need Help?

    How to Apply

    Follow the 4-step wizard: enter your address, choose suspension details, select bays, then review and pay.

    Address Step

    Select a saved address or search for a new one. If you can't find it, tick "Enter manually" to type it in yourself.

    Suspension Details

    Choose a reason, pick your start date (at least 5 working days ahead), and set the duration. The end date will calculate automatically.

    Bay Details

    Select the street and bay type, enter the number of spaces, and add any vehicle registration numbers. Pricing updates in real time.

    Checkout

    Review your application summary, enter payment details, and submit. You'll receive a confirmation with a reference number.

    Accessibility

    Use the A / A+ / A++ buttons in the header to adjust text size. Your preference is saved automatically.
